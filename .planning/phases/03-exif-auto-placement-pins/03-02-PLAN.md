<objective>
Deliver the pins-and-clustering drill-down slice: when a user opens a country, render a
self-contained Leaflet map inside the sidebar showing a pin at each geolocated photo's
exact coordinates, with nearby pins clustered and expandable on zoom, and a pin popup that
opens the existing lightbox. Covers GEO-03, GEO-04, and the manual-fallback half of GEO-05
(GPS-less photos still appear in the gallery, just without pins).

Purpose: This makes the stored coordinates visible — the payoff of Phase 3's auto-placement.
Output: the `location` field surfaced through `usePhotos`, the `PhotoPinMarker` / `ClusterMarker`
/ `PinPopup` DivIcon components, the `CountryPinMap` MapContainer, and its wiring into
`CountrySidebar` (with the country bbox threaded from `WorldMap`).
</objective>

## Phase Goal

**As a** PhotoMap user, **I want to** open a country and see my photos pinned at their exact GPS spots with nearby ones clustered, **so that** I can visually explore where inside a country each photo was taken and jump to any photo from its pin.
